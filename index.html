(good sex is safe sex)

a social campaign for college students to practice safer sex

sexually transmitted infections are more common in youths aged 15-24. however, existing safe sex campaigns often fail to resonate with young adults, presenting in an either overly serious or campy manner. (good sex is safe sex) aims to remind young adults of the importance of using protection, avoiding gendered or strictly heterosexual overtones.
